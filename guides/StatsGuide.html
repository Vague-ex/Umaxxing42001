Help & Guide

Everything you need to know about planning your gacha pulls

About This Website

This website is designed for Uma Musume Pretty Derby players who struggle to decide which banners to pull on and how to manage their carrats (gacha currency).

Our calculator helps you plan your pulls by calculating your daily carrat income and projecting how many carrots you'll have by the time your desired banner arrives!

Features

Daily Calculator

Calculate your daily carrat income from various sources including login bonuses, missions, and events.

Weekly PVP Trials

Track your weekly carrot income from Team Trials (PVP) and include it in your calculations.

Banner Timeline

Select your target banner and the calculator will estimate how many carrots you'll accumulate by that date.

Save & Load

Save your calculations and load them later. Never lose track of your planning!

How to Use

  1. Enter Your Current Carrots: Input how many carrots you currently have in your account.
  2. Set Daily Income: Enter your daily carrot income from dailies, missions, and login bonuses.
  3. Add Weekly PVP: Include your weekly Team Trials rewards (converted to daily average).
  4. Select Target Banner: Choose which upcoming banner you want to pull on.
  5. View Results: See the projected number of carrots you'll have by the banner date!
  6. Save Your Plan: Use the save feature to keep your calculations for future reference.

Tips & Tricks

Pro Tip: Remember to update your daily income when new events start or when you rank up in Team Trials!
  • Always add a buffer to your calculations - unexpected events can give bonus carrots!
  • Check the Japanese server timeline (4 years ahead) to plan for future banners.
  • Consider saving carrots for limited banners rather than permanent ones.
  • Don't forget to include event rewards in your calculations.
