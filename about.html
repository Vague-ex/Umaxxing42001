About Umapilled Project

Your comprehensive resource hub for Uma Musume Pretty Derby

Our Mission

The Umapilled Project is designed to help Uma Musume Pretty Derby players navigate the complex world of horse girl racing, gacha mechanics, and competitive gameplay.

We believe that every trainer should have access to clear, organized information and powerful tools to make informed decisions about their resources, team compositions, and progression strategies.

Why "Umapilled"? Because once you're invested in Uma Musume, there's no going back—you're officially "pilled" on horse girl excellence.

Project Overview

The Umapilled Project consists of two main pillars: Guides for learning and Tools for planning. Together, they form a complete ecosystem for both new and experienced players.

Educational Guides

Comprehensive written resources covering every aspect of the game, from beginner basics to advanced strategies.

Interactive Tools

Practical calculators and planners to help you manage resources, plan pulls, and optimize your gameplay.

Guides Library

Our guides are organized to help players at every stage of their Uma Musume journey:
