Using the Banner Planner

The Banner Planner is one of our most popular tools. Here's how to use it effectively:

Set Your Current Resources

Enter your current carrats in the calculator. Be honest with your count—this is your starting point for all calculations.

Tip: Include both free and paid carrats if you plan to spend money.
Configure Income Sources

Set your Arena Class, weekly outcome, and any special events. These values determine your daily/weekly income.

Tip: If you hover between classes, use the "Hovering between Class 5 and 6" toggle for averaged income.
Browse and Select Banners

Use the filters to find upcoming banners. You can filter by year, type (character/support), or search by name.

Tip: Toggle "Show Missed Banners" to see past banners for reference.
Set Your Target

Click the star icon on any banner to set it as your target. The calculator will automatically update with days until that banner.

Tip: Your target is saved locally, so it will persist between visits.
Understanding the Results
  • Days until banner: How many days until your target banner starts
  • From dailies: Carrots from daily missions and login bonuses
  • From weeklies: Weekly mission rewards
  • From PvP rewards: Arena/Trial rewards based on your class
  • Special events & gifts: Any additional income you specified
  • Total carrats: Your projected total on banner day

Tips & Best Practices

Pro Tip: Always add a 10-20% buffer to your Banner Planner calculations for unexpected events or maintenance rewards.
  • Update regularly: Revisit the Banner Planner weekly to adjust for any changes in your Arena rank or event participation.
  • Use filters: The banner timeline has powerful filters—use them to focus on specific character types or time periods.
  • Check external resources: Our External Resources guide links to Japanese databases that show banners 4 years ahead.
Common Mistake: Forgetting to include event rewards in your special events calculation. Check current and upcoming events regularly!
  • Save targets: The Banner Planner saves your target locally—use this to track progress toward multiple banners.
  • Combine guides: Use the Character Guide with the Scenario Guide for optimal training paths.
  • Seasonal adjustments: Some tools (like PvP) may need adjustment based on current meta or season.

Troubleshooting & FAQ

Try these steps:
  1. Check if you have "Show Missed Banners" toggled off while viewing past dates
  2. Clear any search filters that might be hiding banners
  3. Try selecting "All Years" in the year filter
  4. Refresh the page - sometimes the banner data needs to reload

The calculator provides estimates based on:
  • Your current carrat count
  • Configured daily/weekly income
  • Days until the target banner
Note: Actual results may vary due to:
  • Unexpected events or maintenance rewards
  • Changes in your Arena rank
  • Special login campaigns
  • Global banner schedule variations
Always add a buffer of 10-20% to your calculations.

Currently, the Banner Planner allows you to set one target banner at a time. However, you can:
  • Save your current configuration
  • Calculate for one banner, note the results
  • Reset and calculate for another banner
  • Compare the results manually
We're working on a multi-banner planning feature for future updates!

Our banner timeline is based on the Japanese server schedule, which is approximately 4 years ahead of the global version. However:
  • Global sometimes gets banners in a different order
  • Timing may be adjusted for global events/holidays
  • Some banners may be skipped or combined
Use our timeline as a general guide for what's coming, but always check official announcements for exact global dates.

You need 200 pulls (equivalent to 20,000 carrats) to hit hard pity for a guaranteed character. The Banner Planner will show you how close you are to this goal based on your target banner date.
Remember: This is for the spark/pity system. You could get the character earlier, but 200 pulls guarantees at least one copy.
